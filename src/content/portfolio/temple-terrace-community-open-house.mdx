## About the event

The Temple Terrace Community Open House brings residents together with local people and organizations helping shape the city’s future. Guests can share what matters to them, discover ways to get involved, and enjoy light refreshments and food.

The event takes place Tuesday, June 30, 2026, from 6:30 to 8:30 PM at the Omar K. Lightfoot Center, 10901 North 56th Street, Temple Terrace, Florida.

I’ll be photographing the event and producing a video for YouTube. The completed photo gallery and video will be added to this page after the event.
