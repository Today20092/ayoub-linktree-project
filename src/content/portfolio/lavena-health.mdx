## Overview

I produce Lavena Wellness's podcast and help turn each conversation into a steady stream of social media content. The work covers the full production cycle, from recording clean dialogue with Shure MV7+ microphones to delivering polished long-form episodes and short-form reels.

## Podcast production

For each recording, I handle the technical production, audio engineering, video editing, and color grading. Episodes are edited and finished in DaVinci Resolve for publication on Lavena Wellness's YouTube channel.

## Social media content

The podcast also serves as a content engine for Lavena's social platforms. Using OpusClip AI as part of the editing workflow, I identify strong moments from each conversation and develop more than 30 social media reels from a single episode.

This is an ongoing creative partnership, with more podcast episodes and social-first content currently in production.
