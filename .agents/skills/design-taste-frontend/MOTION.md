# Motion

Choose motion after the static hierarchy works. Motion may reveal content order, preserve spatial continuity between states, confirm an action, or create a single memorable brand moment. If it has none of those jobs, remove it.

Match motion to the declared intensity dial. At low intensity, use instant or short state feedback. At medium intensity, use restrained entrance and interaction transitions. At high intensity, choreograph only a few focal moments and maintain a calm reading path.

Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`: eliminate nonessential transforms, autoplay, parallax, and looping decoration. Keep duration and easing consistent with the shape and brand language. Test keyboard, touch, and reduced-motion behavior.
